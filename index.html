About

I’m an Italian developer based in Málaga, with around five years of experience building production software for insurance and industrial environments.

Python has always been my main language. I enjoy working on APIs, integrations and systems where readable code and clear boundaries matter. More recently, I built Document Intelligent Hub to deepen my work with FastAPI, retrieval and private document knowledge.

The past year has also been a personal transition: relocating to Málaga and studying Spanish intensively. Outside software, I’m involved in animal-rights street activism, calisthenics and Dungeons & Dragons.
